The problem with medical education

The pace of change in medicine is accelerating, and physicians are struggling to keep up.
Every year, doctors spend millions of hours sifting through articles and flying to CME conferences to collect continuing medical education credit. That’s time that could be spent with patients, family and friends.
The result: increasing physician burnout and missed opportunities to improve our collective health.

How we’re fixing it

We provide curated clinical knowledge efficiently and effectively so that physicians can get back to what matters: being the best doctors they can be.

Why it works

Our education technology combines content from elite clinical professionals with adult learning innovations from educational experts in an online platform personalized to clinicians’ learning needs and accessible wherever and whenever they are.
